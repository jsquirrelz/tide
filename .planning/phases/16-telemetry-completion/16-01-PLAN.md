| T-16-02 | Denial of Service | PrometheusHandler upstream call | mitigate | This plan's TELEM-06 fix: `http.Client{Timeout: 30s}` + `NewRequestWithContext(r.Context())` — browser disconnects cancel upstream work; hangs are bounded. |
