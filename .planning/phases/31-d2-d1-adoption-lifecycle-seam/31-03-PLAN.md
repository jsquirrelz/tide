<objective>
Implement D1 idempotency: make child-level (milestone / phase / plan) planner
budget rollup exactly-once by gating each `budget.RollUpUsage` call on a durable
per-level marker — `MilestoneRolledUpUID` / `PhaseRolledUpUID` / `PlanRolledUpUID`
(D-03 level-specific names) — mirroring the project-level Phase-27 pattern. Today
the child rollups gate only on `isFirstCompletion`, which flips back to true once
the reporter Job TTL-GCs (300s) — causing the double-count that corrupts
`CostSpentCents` on halt→resume. The project-level rollup (and the adoption
advance) ship in plans 02/01; this plan closes the child-level exactly-once
guarantee so spend that accrues under an adopted Project (ADOPT-02) is also
correct (ADOPT-04).

Purpose: Removes the non-idempotent-reconcile anti-pattern at the three child
levels; guarantees the budget meter is trustworthy under TTL-GC.
Output: Marker-gated rollups in three controllers; new envtest.
</objective>
