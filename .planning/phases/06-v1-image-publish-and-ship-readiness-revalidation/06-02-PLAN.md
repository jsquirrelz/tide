<objective>
Add `FROM --platform=$BUILDPLATFORM` to all 6 component Dockerfile builder stages and fix the 4 alpine images' missing `ARG TARGETARCH` + `GOARCH=${TARGETARCH}` in their `go build` RUN.

Purpose: This is the load-bearing cross-compile fix for IMG-01. Without `--platform=$BUILDPLATFORM`, buildx arm64 builds run the `go build` step under QEMU emulation (slow, flaky). With it, the native amd64 runner cross-compiles Go to arm64 in seconds. The alpine images additionally need `GOARCH=${TARGETARCH}` because their current `go build` only passes `GOOS=linux` â€” they would produce amd64 binaries for arm64 images without this fix.

Output: All 6 Dockerfiles updated; `docker buildx build --platform linux/amd64,linux/arm64` works correctly for each.
</objective>
